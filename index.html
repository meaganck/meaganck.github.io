Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3 bananas
  • 1/3 cup of melted butter
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1 tsp of choice of spice, like cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 cup of white or milk chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and grease pan
  2. Add bananas to a medium sized bowl, and mash them
  3. Add melted butter to bananas and stir well
  4. Add sugar, baking soda, spice, vanilla, salt, flour and egg to bananas
  5. Add chocolate chips to bowl, and lightly stir.
  6. Pour mixture into greased pan, and add some chocolate chips on top
  7. Bake for about 50 mins to an hour
  8. Enjoy some warm banana bread!
